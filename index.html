About

Breva Solutions provides and distributes security products and services predominetley in the East African region.

Our mission is to ensure clients can operate safely in high-risk countries by providing them with the necessary tools.

Our value proposition includes; high-end security products such as forensic marking and on-site risk management training.

  • Breva Solutions

    Global Network

    Our portfolio of solutions are built to suit a wide range of customers. We have strong parnership relationships with global vendors for the highest quality products. We protect consumers, small companies, medium-sized businesses and enterprises from different types of threats and provide them with convenient tools to control and manage their security.

  • Kenya | Nairobi

    Breva Solutions' cosmopolitan business hub

    Nairobi has experienced one of the highest growth rates of any city in Africa. Kenya has expanded its economy with information and communication showing a steady increase. Breva Solution is present in East Africa’s tech capital working with experts who strive to solve East Africa’s security issues.

  • Uganda | Kampala

    The 13th fastest growing city on the planet

    Breva Solutions has focused on the Ugandan market with expected growth in 2019 to increase by 55% as we secure contracts within this market. Currently Foreign Direct Investment (FDI) in Uganda is at an all time high and the numbers are expected to increase within the next 5 years. The construction industry in Uganda is booming and the discovery of oil has been projected to increase FDI.

  • Rwanda | Kigali

    Most aspirational African city

    A recent'Smart Rwanda' initiative and master plan has been published with goals set for 2040 objectives. Kigali has experienced huge foreign investments in its economy. Its devotion to provide technology service to its population makes it an attractive country for investment firms. Breva Solutions is positioned to support new businesses in Rwanda with security solutions and services.

  • Tanzania | Dar es Salaam

    Huge port serving 8 land-locked countries.

    Although the capital city is Dodoma, the largest city is Dar es Salaam with huge potential in terms of foreign investment. Recently Tanzania has discovered 2.17 trillion cubic feet of natural gas reserves. The region has also planned to expand Bagamoyo port to increase capacity to handle 20 million containers in order to increase foreign investment and the countries productivity. Breva Solutions is seizing this as an opportunity to leverage it’s services within this geographic region offering our portfolio of security products and services.
