About

Breva Solutions provides and distributes security products and services prodominetley in the East African region.

Our mission is to ensure clients can operate safely in high risk countries by providing them with the necessary tools.

Our value proposition includes; high-end security products such as forensic marking, and on-site risk management training.

  • United Kingdom

    Headquartered in London

    Global security experts provide innovative solutions via our London HQ. With the right mix of technical, regional and business know-how we can repond quickly to security needs.

  • Kenya | Nairobi

    The cosmopolitan business hub

    Nairobi has experienced one of the highest growth rates of any city in Africa. Breva Security is present in this market as your trusted security partner.

  • Uganda | Kampala

    The 13th fastest growing city on the planet

    Construction in Uganda is booming. Breva Security has positioned itself to secure any business assets and inventory in this fast-paced location

  • Rwanda | Kigali

    Most aspirational African city

    With a master plan for 2040, Kigali is seeing huge foreign investments in its economy. Breva Security is positioned to assist businesses in Rwanda with security solutions.

  • Tanzania | Dar es Salaam

    Huge port serving 8 land-locked countries.

    Although the capital city is Dodoma, the largest city is Dar es Salaam with huge potential in terms of foreign invesment.
