Cyber Security Solutions & Online Training

Suffering from a phishing, malware or ransomware cyber-attack can be catastrophic, financially burdensome and damaging to brand reputation. Upskilling staff in how to identify these risks is a more efficient and effective way to deal with these risks before they occur. We offer End-User Security Awareness (EUSA) training, which aims to help organisations change end-user behaviour and achieve compliance.

High Security Applications for Everyday Devices

Mobile usage in East Africa is growing. We ensure companies are able to communicate securely and effortlessly, by providing a superior after-sales service with our trusted suppliers. Distribution, installation and maintenance services are all part of the Breva Solutions offering.

Hardware Security & Data Management Solutions

We provide Hardware Security Modules (HSMs) which are key to generating and storing passwords, certificates and encryption keys in a tamper-proof way. Certificates and passwords are the “keys to the kingdom” of your organisation; storing them in any other way, seriously compromises your defence against malicious acts. Our solutions compliment applications such as e-Government, database encryption and payments & transactions applications.

About

Breva Solutions provides and distributes security products and services predominantly in the East African region. Our mission is to ensure clients can operate safely in a global environment by providing them with industry-leading tools.

Our value proposition is simple. We provide tools to secure businesses in their daily communications, online presence and physical access.

    Property Marking Technology

We have built a strong relationship with our partners and have been able to support their entry into a fast growing emerging markets, with our hands-on regional support.
